Pin AIVE to the toolbar
  • Click the puzzle-piece icon (Extensions) in the toolbar.
  • Find “All-in-One Video Enhancer (AIVE)”.
  • Click the pin icon so AIVE stays visible.

What these buttons do
  • Pause AIVE on this tab – temporarily turns AIVE’s effects on/off for the current tab only.
  • Disable AIVE on this site – adds this website to AIVE’s internal blocklist so it won’t auto-run here.
  • Enable AIVE on this site – removes this website from the blocklist.
