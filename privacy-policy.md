# All-in-One Video Enhancer (AIVE) Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 2026_

AIVE does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information with the developer.

## Local Settings

AIVE stores extension settings locally in your browser using Chrome's `storage.local` API. These settings can include slider defaults, panel position, panel state, disabled-site hostnames, and blacklist hostnames.

## Page Access

AIVE runs on pages you visit so it can find HTML5 video players and apply visual adjustments. The extension does not send page content, browsing history, video data, or usage activity to the developer.

## No Analytics Or Tracking

AIVE does not include analytics, tracking pixels, telemetry, advertising code, remote logging, or developer-controlled servers.
